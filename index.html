Ningna Wang

Hello, 你好, Bonjour, こんにちは, 안녕하세요!

My name is Ningna Wang (王宁娜 in Chinese). I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Graphics Lab at the University of Texas at Dallas. Under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohu Guo, my research focuses on geometric modeling, especially medial axis transforms.

Prior to UTD, I worked as a Senior Software Developer at Booking.com, Amsterdam, Netherlands.
